🎯 Vision

INIT-185 — Project Final Fantasy In Progress

DFS+ will be built as the most approachable, confidence-building, and fun entry point into the Fanatics ecosystem — a low-friction, games-first experience where casual fans learn, return, and gradually deepen their relationship with sports and Fanatics. Over the next 18–24 months, this initiative will:

• Make DFS+ the product fans “learn on,” with experiences that adapt as users gain confidence so repeat play feels easier, smarter, and more rewarding.
• Shift engagement from event-driven spikes to habitual, routine play (e.g., part of a weekly sports rhythm), with resilient cohorts that persist well beyond major events.
• Use generosity (promos, rewards, DI) in a disciplined, EV-modeled way: helping users overcome the learning curve and build habits early, then evolving into more targeted, loyalty-oriented rewards as the product itself drives stickiness.
• Position DFS+ as a durable, standalone product that serves as a trusted top-of-funnel on-ramp into Markets and Sportsbook, introducing those products in simple, highly engaging ways without overwhelming cross-sell or cannibalization.
• Compete primarily on product quality — clarity, feedback, progression, and social/fun —…
